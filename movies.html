Task #1

The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

The Last Samurai (2003)

The Last Samurai is an American epic period action drama film directed by Edward Zwick. It follows an American military officer, Nathan Algren (played by Tom Cruise), who is hired to train the Japanese army in modern warfare. After being captured by samurai rebels, he becomes deeply influenced by their culture and code of honor.

The Patriot (2000)

The Patriot was directed by Roland Emmerich. The film follows Benjamin Martin (Mel Gibson), a farmer and former soldier, who is reluctantly drawn into the American Revolutionary War after his family is torn apart by British forces.

The 13th Warrior (1999)

The 13th Warrior (1999) was directed by John McTiernan. The film follows an Arab ambassador, Ahmad ibn Fadlan (Antonio Banderas), who is sent to the north to join a group of Viking warriors. Together, they face a mysterious and deadly enemy in a battle for survival
